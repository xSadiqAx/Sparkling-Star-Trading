SERVICES WE OFFER

At Sparkling Star Trading, our unwavering commitment to quality, precision, and expertise shines brightly across our specialized metal fabrication services. Each project we undertake is a testament to our dedication and passion for the craft, catering to diverse industries and clients.

ALUMINIUM


At Sparkling Star Trading, our expertise covers Cast Aluminium Gates, Fencing, Staircases, Balustrades, and Grills. Harnessing technology, we excel in Automated Car Park Systems, Gates, Tempered Glass Solutions, Spider Fittings, and Overhead Section Doors. Our commitment ensures each piece stands out in design and function, showcasing our craftsmanship.

MILD STEEL


Sparkling Star Trading is renowned for Mild Steel Fabrication. With its distinctive Carbon content (0.05-0.25%), Mild Steel is transformed in our hands. Our seasoned team crafts for sectors like chemicals, electrics, cement, and more. From Handrails, Staircases, Gates, and Grills to Tanks and Chumminess, our services resonate quality and precision, a testament to our legacy.

STAINLESS STEEL
